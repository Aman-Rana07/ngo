ABOUT US

Kind Beings is a government-registered non-governmental organization founded in November 2018, based in New Delhi, India. We are a collective of young professionals driven by a shared purpose to create meaningful social impact through consistent acts of kindness. What began as a small volunteer initiative has grown into a fast-rising grassroots organization working across multiple states in India. We focus on addressing real- world challenges through action-oriented, community-driven programs.

At Kind Beings, we work across key impact areas including education, youth and women empowerment, hunger relief, animal welfare, and environmental care. Our efforts are rooted in empathy, collaboration, and long-term commitment to the communities we serve.

Over the years, we have partnered with reputed institutions and organizations such as HCL, Dell, NDIM, FIENA, World Animal Protection, and others to deliver measurable, on-ground impact.

We don’t just help people — we stand with them in their journey toward dignity, independence, and a better future. Because for us, kindness is not just what we do — it is who we are.
