ABOUT US

Kind Beings is a government-registered non-governmental organization founded in November 2018, based in New Delhi, India. We are a collective of young professionals driven by a shared purpose to create meaningful social impact through consistent acts of kindness. What began as a small volunteer initiative has grown into a fast-rising grassroots organization working across multiple states in India. We focus on addressing real- world challenges through action-oriented, community-driven programs.

At Kind Beings, we work across key impact areas including education, youth and women empowerment, hunger relief, animal welfare, and environmental care.

Over the years, we have partnered with reputed institutions and organizations such as HCL, Dell, NDIM, FIENA, World Animal Protection, and others.

We don't just help people, we stand with them. Because for us, kindness is not just what we do it is who we are.

MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

To create sustainable social impact by addressing real community needs through education, empowerment, compassion, and action. We strive to enable individuals and communities to live with dignity by building opportunities, nurturing self-belief, and promoting a culture of kindness through consistent grassroots efforts.

Our Vision

We envision an inclusive and compassionate society where every individual has access to basic necessities, equal opportunities, and the freedom to grow without barriers. A future where kindness is not occasional, but a way of life — shaping communities, inspiring change, and creating lasting impact across generations.

We encourage you to contribute through Volunteering and help us make the world a better place one step at a time .
