Achievements

From a humble spark in 2018 to a bigger movement, our journey has been defined by an ever-growing circle of compassion. What began with just ten dedicated volunteers in a single state has flourished into a powerful network of over 2,000 changemakers spanning nine states. Over the last eight years, we have scaled our reach from touching 200 lives to empowering more than 6,000 beneficiaries, proving that purpose-driven growth knows no bounds. As we look toward 2026 and beyond, we remain committed to the belief that as our family grows, so does our ability to foster a kinder, stronger, and more resilient world for everyone.

  • Recognized as one of Delhi’s fastest-growing NGOs
  • Partnered with leading organizations like HCL, DELL, Young Indians, NDIM, FIENA, Those in Need, World Animal Protection and more
  • Reached and supported 6000+ beneficiaries across 9 states in just 4 years
    • Built a strong community of 2000+ young volunteers driving real social impact
  • Provided vocational & skill-development programs to help volunteers grow personally and professionally
  • Conducted one-on-one mentorship sessions for youth career guidance and confidence-building
  • Championed animal welfare initiatives, supporting and caring for voiceless beings
  • Led environmental, youth empowerment, and community development projects
  • Strengthened the culture of compassion through youth-driven acts of kindness nationwide
